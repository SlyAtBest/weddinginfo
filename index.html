Information

Little Ones & Youngsters

SADLY DUE TO A RESTRICTION ON NUMBERS WE WON'T BE ABLE TO ACCOMMODATE ANY LITTLE ONES OR YOUNGSTERS AT OUR WEDDING OR RECEPTION, EXCEPT THOSE INVOLVED IN THE WEDDING PARTY. WE APPRECIATE THIS MAY MAKE IT IMPOSSIBLE OR VERY DIFFICULT FOR SOME OF YOU TO ATTEND, BUT IF YOU ARE ABLE TO FIND SOMEONE WHO CAN LOOK AFTER YOUR LOVED ONES FOR A DAY OR TWO WE WOULD LOVE IT IF YOU COULD JOIN US FOR OUR BIG DAY.

Parking & Disabled Access

THERE IS A DEDICATED CAR PARK AT THE VENUE, SO FINDING A SPACE SHOULD NOT BE AN ISSUE. GUESTS ARE ALSO ABLE TO LEAVE THEIR VEHICLES ONSITE OVERNIGHT SHOULD THEY WISH AND CAN COLLECT THEM THE NEXT DAY. THE CAR PARK IS SITUATED NOT FAR FROM THE BARN, BUT FOR THOSE REQUIRING DISABLED ACCESS, IT IS POSSIBLE TO DROP OFF EVEN CLOSER IF NEEDED. THE VENUE ITSELF IS ACROSS A SINGLE LEVEL AND A DISABLED TOILET IS AVAILABLE
